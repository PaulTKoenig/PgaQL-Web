Statistical Approach to Predicting NCAAB Outcomes
Developed logistic regression model that predicts the outcome of basketball games at a ~97% accuracy given the final box score and derived advanced stats from NCAA College basketball games.
Developed two team ranking systems based off scoring and overall efficiency, data preprocessing transformation algorithm that significantly reduced model overfitting, and future matchup box score prediction to ~30% accuracy.
When put together, achieved ~73% accuracy in producing the correct outcome of games only using statistics provided before the game.
Outperformed all similar models found with publicly available prediction accuracies including the TeamRanking.com Decision Tree Model (71.2%), a website that is described as "the web's #1 source for algorithmic sports predictions and data".
